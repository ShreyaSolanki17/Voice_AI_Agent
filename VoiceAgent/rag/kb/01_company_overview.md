# BrightBox Company & Product Overview

BrightBox is a home organization subscription box service. Every month, we deliver curated organization tools and storage solutions to help customers declutter and organize their spaces.

**What We Deliver:**
- Monthly subscription box ($39/month) with organization products
- Items include: storage bins, drawer organizers, shelf dividers, labeling kits, cleaning caddies, pantry containers, and seasonal tools
- Free shipping on all boxes

**Delivery & Timing:**
- Boxes ship on the 1st of each month via standard ground shipping
- Delivery takes 3-7 business days after shipping
- Orders placed after the 25th of the month ship the following month
