# Shipping, Returns & Billing FAQ

## Shipping

**Q: When will my box arrive?**
A: Boxes ship on the 1st of each month via standard ground shipping. Delivery takes 3-7 business days after shipping.

**Q: What if I order after the 25th?**
A: Orders placed after the 25th ship the following month's box cycle.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: We currently ship only within the continental United States.

**Q: Can I track my shipment?**
A: Yes, tracking information is emailed when your box ships. You can also find it in your account dashboard.

## Returns & Refunds

**Q: What is your return policy?**
A: You can return any unused items within 30 days for a full refund. Contact support for a prepaid return label.

**Q: How do I start a return?**
A: Email support@brightbox.com or call 1-800-BRIGHT-BOX with your order number. We'll send a prepaid return label.

**Q: Do you offer exchanges?**
A: Yes, exchanges are available for damaged or defective items. Contact support within 14 days of delivery.

## Billing

**Q: When will I be charged?**
A: Your card is charged on the 1st of each month, before the box ships.

**Q: Can I change my billing date?**
A: Billing dates are tied to your signup date and cannot be changed. Contact support if you need special accommodations.

**Q: How do I pause or cancel my subscription?**
A: You can pause or cancel anytime through your account dashboard. Cancellations take effect before the next billing cycle.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Refunds are available for unused returns within 30 days. We do not provide prorated refunds for partial months.
